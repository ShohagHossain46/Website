About

Profile

I'm a Deputy Manager (Grade-6) in the Fuel Management Department at BIFPCL's Maitree Super Thermal Power Plant, working in Coal Handling Plant (CHP) operations with rotating shift-incharge responsibilities across morning, evening, and night shifts.

My work centers on keeping fuel supply reliable and safe — from blending Indonesian coal grades to hold boiler performance within margin, to running accident analysis across conveyors, stackers, reclaimers, and ship unloaders. I was field-level engineer on the commissioning of Bangladesh's first 1320 MW ultra-supercritical coal power plant, and trained on supercritical plant operations at NTPC in India. Earlier roles at Renata Limited (pharmaceutical HVAC & utilities) and Samuda Chemical Complex shaped a disciplined, systems-first approach to plant reliability.
